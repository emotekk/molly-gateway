What is this?

This gateway creates a private, encrypted bridge between your Molly app and Signal's servers using Tailscale (a zero-trust VPN). Your phone can receive push notifications without Google Play Services.

Setup Instructions

  1. Go to Tailscale Admin Console
  2. Click "Generate auth key"
  3. Enable "Reusable" and "Ephemeral" (optional but recommended)
  4. Copy the key (starts with tskey-auth-...)
  5. Paste it below and click Deploy Gateway
