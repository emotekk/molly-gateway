üìã How to Register Molly

Choose whichever method is easiest for you:

  1. Install Tailscale on your phone from Play Store / F-Droid and log in with the same account as your Pi
  2. Keep Tailscale enabled (skip this if using Method 4: Local Network Only)
  3. Open Molly ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí UnifiedPush
  4. Choose a registration method:
    • Method 1 - QR Code: Tap "Scan QR Code" and scan the code above (works from desktop or another device)
    • Method 2 - Direct Link: Tap the "Open Gateway" button, then copy the URL from browser and paste in Molly
    • Method 3 - Copy Tailscale URL: Click "Copy Tailscale URL", then paste in Molly's UnifiedPush settings (works everywhere)
    • Method 4 - Local Network Only: Click "Copy Local URL", then paste in Molly (‚ö†Ô∏è only works on home WiFi - for tablets/devices that never leave home)
  5. ‚úÖ Done! You'll receive notifications (everywhere with Tailscale, home-only with local URL)
